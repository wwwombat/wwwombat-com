I'm **wwwombat** — a cybersecurity student, homelab builder, and aspiring enterprise AI consultant.

I built this site to document what happens when you take consumer hardware, open-source AI models, and a security-first mindset and try to build something that rivals managed cloud services — without handing your data to anyone.

## What I'm Building

**WWWOMBAT.tech** is the commercial side: enterprise AI consulting focused on helping organizations deploy private, governed AI infrastructure. The kind of setup where sensitive data never leaves your perimeter.

**This site** (wwwombat.com) is the lab notebook. I write about what I'm building, what broke, what I learned fixing it, and the tools I'm releasing along the way.

## The Stack

Everything I write about here runs on real hardware I own and operate. The core platform is a self-hosted AI execution environment built on Proxmox, with GPU-accelerated local inference, hardened container orchestration, and zero-trust edge access. No cloud dependencies for core operations.

More details on the [Lab](/lab/) page.
